Inspecalytics Features

Discover the comprehensive suite of tools that Inspecalytics offers to enhance your inspection workflows, data management, and analytical capabilities.

Project Management System

Hierarchical Folder Organization

Organize your projects intuitively with a flexible, multi-level folder structure. Easily manage and locate projects, no matter the scale of your operations.

Project Creation and Management

Create new projects swiftly with customizable templates. Manage ongoing projects with tools for tracking progress, assigning tasks, and collaborating with team members.

Detailed Project Metadata

Capture essential project information, including inspection dates, property details, client information, and custom fields tailored to your specific needs.

Support for Different Project Types

Adapt Inspecalytics to various inspection scenarios. Whether it's building inspections, equipment audits, or safety checks, our platform supports diverse project requirements.

Advanced Inspection Tools

Image-Based Inspection System

Conduct thorough inspections using high-resolution images. Upload, organize, and annotate images directly within the platform for a visual-first approach.

Hotspot/Part Marking Functionality

Precisely identify and mark specific areas of interest or individual parts on your inspection images. Enhance clarity and communication for defect identification and reporting.

Hierarchical Part Organization

Define complex assemblies and components with a hierarchical part structure. Ideal for detailed equipment inspections and manufacturing quality control.

Custom Part Numbering and Color Coding

Implement your own part numbering systems and use color-coding to visually differentiate components, statuses, or priorities within your inspections.

Image Captioning and Annotation

Add detailed captions, notes, and drawn annotations to images. Provide context, highlight issues, and ensure comprehensive documentation for every inspection point.

Type and Subtype Classification

Customizable Type System

Define your own classification types for findings, observations, or assets. Tailor the system to match your industry-specific terminology and reporting standards.

Subtype Categorization with Acronyms

Further refine your classifications with subtypes, complete with customizable acronyms for quick identification and streamlined data entry.

Numeric Value Support for Measurements

Record quantitative data, such as measurements, readings, or scores, directly associated with your inspection types and subtypes.

Hierarchical Type Relationships

Establish parent-child relationships between types and subtypes to create a structured and logical classification hierarchy, improving data consistency and analysis.

Data Management

Secure Authentication System

Protect your valuable inspection data with a robust authentication system, ensuring that only authorized personnel can access and modify information.

Data Export Capabilities

Easily export your project data, reports, and images in various formats (e.g., PDF, CSV, Excel) for offline analysis, client delivery, or integration with other systems.

Project Filtering and Search

Quickly find specific projects or data points using advanced filtering and search functionalities. Sift through large datasets efficiently based on various criteria.

Multi-Level Data Organization

Benefit from a structured approach to data organization that spans projects, folders, parts, types, and images, ensuring clarity and accessibility at every level.
